## About Me

> **Hunting bugs before they hunt users 🐛**

I'm a **QA Engineer / SDET** who lives at the intersection of development and testing. With hands-on experience in **manual and Playwright (JS) automation testing** of web and geospatial applications, I bring a developer's mindset to the testing table — meaning I don't just find bugs, I understand *why* they happen.

- 🔍 Crafting test cases that actually matter
- ⚡ Automating with Playwright & Selenium
- 🌐 Validating APIs like a detective with Postman
- 🗺️ Testing geospatial applications at GeoWGS84
- 🧠 Always thinking: *"What could go wrong here?"*
- 🎯 Detail-obsessed, quality-driven, and endlessly curious
